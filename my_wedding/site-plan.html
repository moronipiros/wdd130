Overview

Purpose

How did you guys meet? Where and when will be your wedding? Is there anything I could help with? That are just some of the questions I received from many people since I get engaged, and I know many other couples receive as well. My goal is to create a website where people will find every needed information about our wedding location, and time, get an invite, know our love history, and even access our gift list if they wish.

Audience

This website is directed to all my family, friends and anyone who wants to know more about my story. How I meet my fianceé, our love story until our marriage, and get information about our cerimony.

Content

Home page

The home page is the reason why people will come to my site, it will contain the story of my fiancée and me, how we met, became friends, and all of our trajectory until today. I expect them to get to know us better than they already know, and feel closer to us in preparation for our wedding. Also, I expect to inspire those that are not married yet to not feel afraid to do so if that is their desire. On this first page, I expect them just to read about our story, and look at the pictures along with the text. Since that is something too personal and unique, there will be no other option to know about us instead of accessing the website or talking directly with us, relatives, and friends.

Images for the Home page

Questions

This page could be the main reason why people will access the site. This page will be way simpler, it will contain the main information to participate in our wedding ceremony, the address (along with a picture of the map linked to the Google Maps website), time, the dress code, and any other necessary information. On this page, the only interaction the user might have is with the map, which redirects the user to Google Maps, to get more information about how to get to the chapel where will be the ceremony.

Images for the Page 2

Gifts

This page is for those who want to gift us with things needed for our home in general. It will include an icon of a gift, that will be also linked to an external site to buy those gifts. That will be a link to a store where will have a list of gifts we may need, and also it will include an image of a QR code that can be scanned to transfer money if they wish to do so instead of buying something directly. They may search for gifts on any site, but only on our website, they will find what we are needing or the QR code to our bank account.
